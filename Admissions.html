Admission Requirements

Documents Needed for Admission

Completed application form - Available for download below or from our office

Birth certificate - Original and one photocopy

Previous school reports - For students transferring from other schools

Passport photos - Two recent color passport-sized photographs

Admission fee - KSh 500 non-refundable application fee

Medical records - Immunization records and any relevant medical information

Note: All documents should be submitted to the admissions office in person or via email to heriwadics@gmail.com

Admission Process

1. Application Submission

Submit the completed application form along with all required documents

Ongoing

2. Review & Assessment

Our admissions team will review your application and may schedule an assessment

Within 2 weeks of submission

3. Admission Decision

You will receive notification of the admission decision via email/phone

Within 1 week after assessment

4. Fee Payment & Registration

Complete the registration process by paying the required fees

Within 2 weeks of acceptance
