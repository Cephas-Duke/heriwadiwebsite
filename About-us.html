Our Journey of Growth

2018 - The Beginning

Heriwadi Christian Schools opened its doors in September 2018 with just two brave students. What started as a vision became reality, laying the foundation for educational excellence in Ongata Rongai.

2019 - Growing Roots

Our second year saw steady growth as we welcomed new families who believed in our mission. From four to seven learners, each addition strengthened our community and reinforced our commitment to excellence.

2020 - Resilience Through Challenge

The global pandemic tested our resolve, but like a phoenix, we emerged stronger. Starting 2020 with nine students, we faced uncertainty with faith and determination, preparing for the breakthrough that was to come.

2021 - The Breakthrough

January 2021 marked our pivotal moment. With five returning students and five new admissions, we reached double digits for the first time. This milestone wasn't just about numbers—it was about trust, growth, and the beginning of our upward trajectory that continues today.
